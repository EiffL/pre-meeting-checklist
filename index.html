Checklist for the Upcoming LSST DESC Meeting

Here’s a few useful things you can do to prepare yourself for the upcoming DESC meeting at CMU!

Check out the agenda page on Confluence. Bookmark this link in your browsers and phones for easy access. Make sure you remember your Confluence password.
Join the Slack channel #desc-collab-meeting to hear announcements and to chat with meeting participants (both in-person and virtual). Also join #desc-hack-sprint if you are going to participate in the Friday Hack Day.
Make sure you have a NERSC account and you remember your password (maybe you use SSH keys, but note that you do need your password to use NERSC's jupyter notebook)!
Log in to Cori and install DESC’s jupyter kernels.
(For DE School participants) To make the most of the 3x2 session, please consider installing the Core Cosmology Library (CCL) in your laptops or familiarising yourself with the NERSC installation. Check out the DE School page for more details.
(For Hack Day participants) Visit our Hack Day Confluence page to browse and add hack/sprint ideas! Remember, not all hacks and sprints have to be carried out, so be bold when adding ideas! Also add your name and departure time at the bottom at the page!
(For virtual participants) Zoom connection instructions are consolidated at [link?]. If you want to be heard in plenary sessions, please raise your hand in Zoom or use the Slack channel ( #desc-collab-meeting) and our coordinators will announce your presence. Please request coordinators for the parallel sessions if you need them.
Remind yourselves of our Code of Conducts and also the Virtual Meeting Best Practices. Note that many of the Best Practices apply to non-virtual meetings as well.
To make sure you will be free from technical difficulties, check out our Tech Cheat Sheet and bookmark it!
If you will be driving or will need a ride, visit the carpooling page [link?] to provide/request rides.
If you use social media professionally, we will be using the #lsstdesc hashtag when reporting highlights from the meeting!
This site uses cookies to keep track your checklist progress. The data are stored in your browser locally only.
